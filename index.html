What do you guys do?

We are a nonprofit start-up looking to water a plant of inspiration in everyone. Whether its hiking, sightseeing, bird-watching, hunting, or just walking through the woods, we want to help nurture a commitment to the outdoors.

How do you guys do it?

By developing strong software and pairing it with excellent hardware, we offer a patent-pending interface-algorithm that integrates nature into everyday life. In other words, we are basically AI-REI.

What is an "eco-profile"?

"Eco-profile" is our patent-pending term used to illustrate the microcosm of life within nature that we cultivate for you. In other words, we create and grow a small piece of nature for you to then experience, all driven by our AI - Sally.
